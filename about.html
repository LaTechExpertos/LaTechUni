About LaTech University

University of LaTech is an online university offering Computer based courses. The University has been set up to provide quality education in the field of computer education, as well as business management, information technology and allied subjects. The University provides training to students who wish to obtain higher degrees in these subjects.

The University's objective is to provide training that can be thoroughly delivered via internet, which will allow students to benefit from the convenience of working from home or anywhere else they choose without having to travel long distances. This will also enable students to save on travel expenses.

The University offers courses taught by qualified teachers using a computer based learning platform that provides very effective communication between students and their teachers. The platform allows students to interact with each other through instant messaging and video conferencing facilities at no cost whatsoever for anyone who wishes them!
